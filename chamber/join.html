Business/Organization Description

Non-Profit Membership

  • Non-Profit Networking: Opportunities to connect with other non-profit organizations and potential donors or partners.
  • Grant and Funding Alerts: Regular updates on grant opportunities, funding sources, and resources available for non-profit organizations.
  • Capacity Building Workshops: Access to workshops and training sessions specifically tailored to the needs of non-profit organizations, covering topics such as fundraising, volunteer management, and board development.
  • Collaboration Opportunities: Facilitation of partnerships and collaborations with other non-profits and businesses to maximize impact and efficiency.
  • Advocacy Support: Assistance with advocacy efforts and representation of non-profit interests in relevant policy discussions and decision-making processes.

Gold Membership

  • VIP Access: Exclusive invitations to VIP events, dinners, and receptions with prominent business leaders, politicians, and influencers.
  • Executive Roundtables: Participation in exclusive executive roundtable discussions focused on industry trends and strategic insights.
  • Legislative Advocacy: Representation and advocacy on behalf of your business interests at the local, regional, and national levels.
  • Premium Exposure: Priority placement in the Chamber's marketing materials, including logo placement and sponsor recognition.
  • Tailored Support: Customized support from Chamber staff to address your specific business needs and challenges.

Silver Membership

  • Enhanced Networking Opportunities: Priority access to premium networking events and exclusive meetups with key industry players.
  • Marketing Support: Promotion of your business through the Chamber's social media channels, website, and newsletters.
  • Member Spotlight: Opportunity to be featured in the Chamber's communications, showcasing your business to a wider audience.
  • Advisory Services: Access to advisory services such as business mentoring and consulting to help address specific challenges or opportunities.
  • Additional Discounts: Additional discounts on Chamber services, events, and partner offerings.

Bronze Membership

  • Networking Events: Access to regular networking events where you can meet fellow members and potential business partners.
  • Member Directory: Inclusion in the Chamber's member directory, allowing other businesses and individuals to find and connect with you.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Discounted or free entry to educational workshops and seminars on topics relevant to business growth and development.
  • Newsletter Subscription: Receive a subscription to the Chamber's newsletter, keeping you informed about industry news, events, and opportunities.
  • Access to Resources: Access to a variety of resources such as business templates, guides, and toolkits to assist with various aspects of running your business.
