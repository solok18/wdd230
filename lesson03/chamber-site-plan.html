Sacramento Chamber of Commerce. The city for which the commerce will work to help it grow.

Purpose

The Sacramento Chamber of Commerce is the forefront leader, economic starter, and community go getter. We are here to promote business with our unique approach to public policy, community and economic development. We provide a wide variety of services for our members to help them grow and prosper.

Scenario

How do you help the community grow? In what manners do you promote the local businesses? How do you help to maintain policies that will help local businesses?
